FAQ

Baby shower decoration FAQs

Can you match a baby shower theme?

Yes. Share your colour palette, inspiration images, or theme and we can adjust signage, florals, balloons, and decor pieces.

Can you add gender reveal details?

Yes. We can include gender reveal-friendly styling, signage, balloons, and focal areas depending on the reveal plan.

How long do you need on site?

Setup time depends on the package and venue access, but most baby shower setups need a clear setup window before guests arrive.
