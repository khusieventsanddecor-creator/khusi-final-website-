FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers for Sydney families planning birthday, baby shower, and pasni decoration.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 4–6 weeks ahead for weekend dates, especially during pasni and birthday peak months. We do our best to accommodate shorter notice — message us with your date and we will confirm availability.

Which Sydney suburbs do you deliver to?

We deliver across Greater Sydney including Parramatta, Blacktown, Strathfield, Homebush, Rockdale, Liverpool, and surrounding areas. Delivery is quoted separately based on venue location and setup complexity.

What's included in a Khusi decoration package?

Every package includes the styled backdrop, decor elements listed in that tier, full on-site setup, and pack-down after the event. Delivery is quoted separately. Customisations (extra florals, signage, colour swaps) can be added on request.

Can you style at home, in a hall, or at a venue?

Yes — we style at private homes, community halls, and licensed venues. Please share venue access details (parking, lift, setup window) when booking.

Do you specialise in Nepalese ceremonies?

Yes. Khusi was built around Nepalese family celebrations — pasni (rice-feeding), chudakarma, baby showers, and milestone birthdays — with cultural details respected throughout.

How do I confirm a booking?

After your free consultation, a 30% deposit secures your date. The balance is due one week before the event.

Can the package be customised?

Yes — colour palette, signage text, floral choices, plinth count, and add-ons (candles, cake stand, welcome board) are all customisable. Send your ideas with the enquiry form.
