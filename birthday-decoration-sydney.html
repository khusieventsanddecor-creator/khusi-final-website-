FAQ

Birthday decoration FAQs

Can you create a themed birthday setup?

Yes. We can customise colours, signage, florals, balloons, and feature pieces around the birthday theme.

Do you style first birthdays?

Yes. First birthdays are one of our core milestone events, with child-friendly focal setups and family photo areas.

Can birthday decor be outdoors?

Sometimes. Outdoor setups depend on weather, wind, access, ground surface, and venue rules. We will advise what is safe and practical.
